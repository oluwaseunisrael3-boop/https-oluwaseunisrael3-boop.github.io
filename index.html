Who We Are

Vision & Mission

👁️

Our Vision

A Church Clothed in Authority, Walking in Dominion

To see every believer arise into the fullness of their God-given identity — walking in genuine kingdom authority, operating in the power of the Holy Spirit, and transforming every sphere of human society with the culture of heaven. We envision a generation that does not beg for what God has already given, that does not shrink before what Christ has already defeated, and that does not hide what heaven has already commissioned.

🎯

Our Mission

Equipping. Empowering. Establishing.

Gozom International exists to equip believers with the Word of God in its full depth and power, to empower them by the Holy Spirit for supernatural ministry, and to establish them in their kingdom identity and assignment. Through preaching, teaching, prophetic ministry, discipleship, and the written Word, we are raising a generation of sons and daughters who know their Father, know their enemy, and know their authority — and who live accordingly, every single day.

🔥 Latest Teaching

Walking in the Fullness of Kingdom Authority

📖 Ephesians 2:6 — "And raised us up together and made us sit together in heavenly places in Christ Jesus."
God has not merely saved you from something. He has seated you somewhere. The language of Ephesians 2:6 is present tense — you are seated right now in heavenly places. Far above every principality, every power, every name that is named. This is not a future promise. It is a present reality. The question is not whether God has given you authority. The question is whether you know it, believe it, and are walking in it. Today, change your posture. Stop standing beneath the enemy as though you are at the same level. You are not. You are seated above. Enforce from that position.

🙏 Prayer for Today

Lord, open my eyes to see where You have seated me. Let every prayer I pray today come from the posture of one who is above, not beneath. In Jesus name, Amen.
